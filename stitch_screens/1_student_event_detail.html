Advanced Systems Architecture: Scaling Global Infrastructures

calendar_today October 24 - 26, 2023
location_on Virtual Campus & Amsterdam HQ

About this Event

This intensive three-day summit brings together world-class architects and senior engineers to explore the frontiers of modern system design. We will dive deep into distributed consensus algorithms, high-availability patterns for multi-cloud deployments, and the evolution of edge computing in enterprise environments.

Participants will engage in hands-on architectural design sessions, analyzing real-world failure modes and constructing resilient frameworks capable of handling millions of concurrent requests. This event is part of the Senior Leadership Track and provides essential credits for the Global Enterprise Architect certification.
